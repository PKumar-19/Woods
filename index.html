Frequently Asked Questions

Where is The Woods Kasauli located and how accessible is it? +

Located in Village Bhalori, Shamaghat, Kasauli (Solan District, HP - 173236). It's 60-70 km from Chandigarh Airport (1.5-2 hours), 30 km from Kalka Railway Station, and just 10 km from Dharampur Heritage Railway Station.

What is the villa configuration and total number of units? +

The Woods Kasauli offers only 10 exclusive 4 BHK luxury villas. Each villa spans 3 floors: Ground Floor (living, dining, kitchen, 1 bedroom), First Floor (2 bedrooms, lounge), and Second Floor (1 bedroom, gym space, open & covered terraces). Every villa includes a private lawn and covered parking for 2+ vehicles.

What is the current construction status and expected possession date? +

The project is currently under construction with foundations complete and structural work in progress.

What are the key features and finishes included? +

Premium specifications include earthquake-resistant RCC construction, vitrified tiles in living areas, laminated wooden flooring in bedrooms, modular kitchen with granite countertops, European WC with chrome fittings, UPVC/aluminum glazed windows, and acrylic emulsion walls throughout.

What amenities are provided in the community? +

Multi-level clubhouse with gymnasium and indoor games, 24/7 CCTV security with panic alarm systems, power backup, purified water supply, rainwater harvesting, professional lighting, fire safety equipment, and beautifully landscaped common areas.

What is the price range and payment plan? +

Flexible construction-linked payment plans are available. Contact our sales team for current offers and detailed payment schedules.

Is the project legally approved, and what is the ownership type? +

Yes, The Woods Kasauli has all necessary approvals from local authorities and complies with Himachal Pradesh building regulations. The property is freehold with clear title. All documentation including sale deed, occupancy certificate, and completion certificate will be provided at possession.

Can I customize my villa, and are they furnished? +

Yes, customization options are available during the construction phase. Villas are delivered semi-furnished with a modular kitchen and wardrobes installed. Full furnishing services are available at additional cost. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

Is this suitable for permanent living or investment purposes? +

Both! The Woods Kasauli is designed for year-round living with all modern amenities, making it perfect as a permanent residence, weekend retreat, or holiday home. The villas also offer excellent rental income potential during peak tourist seasons, making them a sound investment in Kasauli's growing premium property market.

How can I schedule a site visit or get more information? +

Fill out the form on our website or call us at +91 79018 55951 to schedule a site visit
